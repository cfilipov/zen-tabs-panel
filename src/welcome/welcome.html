Welcome to ErgoZen

A command palette for Zen Browser with discoverable chord shortcuts for tabs, workspaces, splits, and more — keyboard or mouse. Here's how to get started.

Restart Zen after this update

This update rewrites ErgoZen's keyboard chord handling. Zen can keep old keyboard handlers alive until the browser restarts, so shortcuts may misbehave immediately after updating.

Open the command palette

Press Ctrl+Option+Space or click the toolbar icon to open the tab panel.

Companion Mods

Optional visual enhancements for your tab bar. Install any that interest you — they can be toggled independently in Zen's settings.
